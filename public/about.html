About Me

Born to First Nation (Algonquin) parents in Nothern Quebec in a small french village. Although it was a french village, the town was bilingual because of tourism Many english speaking tourist would come for fishing and hunting.

Photography has been a passion of mine for more than 10 years now. Focussing mainly in Landscaping. Recently I've begun using a drone. Quite cool!

Although Photography is my favourite hobby, I also enjoy listening to music. More specifically music from the 60's and 70's. Those were the good ole days.
